Crystal Collector Game

How to Play

You will be given a random number at the begining of the game. The goal of the game is to match your score to that number.

You can add points to your score by clicking on one of the crystals. Each crystal has a different value to add to your score. Be careful! If you go past the random number amount, you will lose.
